Features

Get quick tips on how the site works and how you can get the most out of Noctis Alba. Still need help? Reach out to us at contact@noctis-alba.com.

Features List

  • EASILY TELL IF YOUR FRIENDS YOU ARE UP WITHOUT WAKING THEM
    On your Friends Page, under "Friends Status," you will see a purple UP which means your friend is up and wants to connect, or the grey ZZZ, which means they are asleep. 

  • SAFELY KNOW IF YOU ARE FRIENDS ARE UP AND WANT TO CONNECT
    On your Friends Page, you get to pick and choose how each friend views your status. Simply toggle "Your Status" to UP to tell your friend that you are up and want to connect or toggle to ZZZ, so they think you are asleep.

    The site will automatically toggle you to ZZZ after 30 min. to ensure you are not woken up if you happen to fall asleep.

  • EASILY INVITE FRIENDS
    Once you have logged in, click on the INVITE link on the top of the page to invite friends. The more friends you invite the fuller your nights will be.
