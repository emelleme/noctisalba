Features

Features List

  • On your contact list you will see the names of the friends you invited. Names that are purple, indicates they are up! If a friends name is grey they are asleep and don’t want to be contacted. 
  • Use the Invite link in the Main Menu or on the Friends page to send an invitation to join you on Noctis Alba. 
  • We give you easy access to always invite more friends to join you on Noctis Alba. More contacts means more connection, obviously ;)
  • Go to the Help link if you want to contact us, review Terms & conditions and Privacy Policy
  • Under your name on the top right is the Settings link. Use this link to change your profile information, change or update your email and password, or Sign-out of the site
