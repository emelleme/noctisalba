The Goal

How many times have you Spoken to friends or family and said:  “Ugh, I didn’t sleep last night.”  Only to have them reply: “OMG neither did I! I wish I knew you were up as well,  I would have liked to have chatted.” Considering the statistics, it is not surprising if it has happened to you. Most insomniacs or people dealing with bouts of insomnia feel frustrated and tired of the sleepless cycle. 

We have observed that meditation, reading, social media, tv, pills and even moving to another room (assuming you have access), isn’t a realistic way for people with insomnia to consistently fall asleep. These “remedies” can leave insomniacs emotionally drained, frustrated, anxious, and still very tired. 

So we wondered, what if we flipped the idea that people need to fix their insomnia by making Insomnia an opportunity instead of a problem?

Noctis Alba does just that, by making it possible to tell people you are awake and want to talk/text without sending them an alert and possibly waking them. Allowing for the opportunity of connecting and having more meaningful hours in your day, no matter what time it is. At Noctis Alba, we hope to offer night owls, secure meaningful connections.
